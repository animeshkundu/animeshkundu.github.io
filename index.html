Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about my work and approach

What type of projects does Animesh Kundu specialize in?

I specialize in web development tools, browser extensions, and command-line utilities. My projects focus on developer productivity tools, network debugging utilities, and media streaming applications. Notable projects include the Mermaid Editor, HAR/SAZ viewers, and the popular youtube-audio Firefox extension with 167+ stars.

What is Animesh Kundu's most popular open source project?

The youtube-audio Firefox extension is my most popular project with over 167 stars and 39 forks on GitHub. It allows users to listen to audio-only streams from YouTube, saving bandwidth and battery life.

What technologies does Animesh Kundu use?

I primarily work with TypeScript, Python, and JavaScript. I use modern web technologies including React, Monaco Editor, and various browser APIs. My projects often leverage client-side processing for privacy and performance.

Are Animesh Kundu's web applications safe to use with sensitive data?

Yes, many of my web applications like the SAZ Viewer and HAR Viewer are designed to work 100% client-side, meaning your data never leaves your browser. All processing happens locally in your browser, ensuring privacy and security for sensitive network debugging data.
